## Star Product: PU Vegetal

Imperveg's Vegetable Polyurethane is a 100% solid, solvent-free polymer derived from castor oil. Its unique characteristics include:

- **Non-toxic:** No VOCs released during curing. Safe for confined spaces.
- **Biocompatible:** Same material used in cranial prostheses (InCor/FMUSP).
- **MS 888 Certified:** Suitable for drinking water contact.
- **High performance:** 1.8 MPa adhesion (Pull-Off), 365m CO₂ barrier.

## About Tecnoveg

**Tecnoveg** is Imperveg's European subsidiary, established in **Portugal in 2025** as part of the company's international expansion strategy. The choice of Portugal was strategic:

- **Gateway to the EU market**
- **Access to government incentives** (Portugal 2030 program)
- **Alignment with strict EU environmental regulations**
- **Growing demand for sustainable construction** in Europe

Tecnoveg distributes the same Imperveg product line under European certifications, with a focus on sustainable construction, bioconstruction, and energy efficiency markets.
