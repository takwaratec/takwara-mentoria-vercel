## 1. From Artisanal to Industry 4.0

The **Bio-Weld Kit** and the passive treatments form the gateway to high-end bioarchitecture. However, for students and investors who wish to advance toward long-term mentorship and large-scale production, the Takwara Platform has developed a complete engineering ecosystem.

This module presents the concepts of the base technologies developed to scale the production of biocomposites and modular structures, ensuring community technological sovereignty.

## 2. The Biorefinery and the Ecological Kiln (T01 and T02)

The bottleneck for large-scale bamboo production is standardized treatment.

**Ecological Kiln T01:** "Smokeless" system (no toxic smoke) with gas re-combustion for Biochar production. Operates with hyper-efficient burning of straw and bamboo waste, reaching the working point at 452°C with zero visible smoke emission (*Clean Burn*). The heat feeds boilers that inject saturated steam into the bamboo chambers.

**Biorefinery T02:** Indirect pyrolysis reactor. Captures non-condensable gases (*syngas*) and extracts **Pyroligneous Extract** (rich in phenolic compounds with natural antifungal and insecticidal action). The system generates a closed zero-waste cycle: pyrolysis gases become heat, heat becomes steam, treated steam becomes stabilized bamboo.

*Reference: ENG-MEM-T01 and ENG-MEM-T02 — Technical Memorial Series T. MQTF Collection (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18827106).*

## 3. Production Machinery (Series T03 to T07)

The standardization of bamboo pieces on an industrial scale is supported by the following equipment (with protected intellectual property aimed at social-regenerative licensing):

### T03 — Rotary Resin Applicator ("Frangueira")

Multi-axis industrial rotisserie system for controlled curing of polymer coatings on bamboo culms up to 6 meters. The technical differential lies in the **Self-Adjusting Conical End Caps** made of elastomer that attach to the bamboo lumen, driven by gradual-torque ratchets. Includes Airless atomized spraying (Tornado brand) for uniform Bio-Weld application. Allows up to 12 poles to rotate slowly during curing, preventing drips and ensuring a perfectly resin-coated inner wall.

- **Capacity:** 12 simultaneous poles (1–6 m)
- **Motivation:** Dripping and bubble formation due to gravity during the polymer "gel" time
- **Reference:** ENG-MEM-T03 — DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18827106

### T04 — Biocomposite Mixer ("Waterproof Concrete Mixer")

Mixing chamber with **Adjustable Gasket Sealing System** that shields the drive shaft against leaks of highly sticky resin. Introduces a **Pneumatic Spray System (PU Jet)** of the "mist" type, which atomizes resin into micrometric particles over the biomass in turbulence. This ensures uniform chemical encapsulation with only 20–25% resin mass. Drum in 304 Stainless Steel or HDPE-lined to prevent adhesion.

- **Resin:fiber ratio:** 1:4 (25% resin for total encapsulation)
- **Rotation:** 30–45 RPM with pneumatic spraying at 60–100 PSI
- **Reference:** ENG-MEM-T04 — DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18827106

### T05 — Interchangeable Cassette Press

Multi-level hydraulic-mechanical press for forming building elements (bricks, cobogós, roof tiles) from biocomposites. The differential is the **Axial Piston System with Interchangeable Cassettes** — quick-change molds that fit into the master chassis via quick-release locks. Calibrated for a minimum pressure of **10 MPa (100 bar)** , ensuring critical density of **800 kg/m³** for structural stability. Supports electrical resistance for hot pressing.

- **Nominal pressure:** 10 MPa
- **Target biocomposite density:** 800 kg/m³
- **Motorization:** Single-phase hydraulic 3 HP
- **Reference:** ENG-MEM-T05 — DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18827106

### T06 — Vegetal Weld Jigs

Fixing and spatial alignment devices for assembling bamboo trusses and structural systems, specifically for the **Vegetal Weld** technique (chemical anchoring with biopolymers). The industrial model uses a laser-perforated metal matrix table (A36) with quick-release **Toggle Clamps** and articulated axes. Allows 360° rotation and alignment of trusses up to 6 meters. The bifacial system operates as a primary cradle for the T07 Press.

- **Artisanal variant:** Marine plywood workbench with "half-moon" clamps
- **Industrial variant:** Perforated A36 table + articulated quick clamps
- **Reference:** ENG-MEM-T06 — DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18827106

### T07 — Sandwich Press for Panels and OSB

Low pressure / high area press for forming structural biocomposite panels. Operates with **static counterweight locking** and manual mechanical clamps, eliminating expensive hydraulic systems. Movable side retaining guides allow panels up to 3,000 mm. The inner face is lined with release film for ready finish (eliminates heavy sanding). Integrates Airless injection terminals for volumetric filling of expansive resins under mechanical confinement.

- **Maximum format:** 2,440 × 3,000 mm
- **System:** Vibrocast counterweight + mechanical clamps
- **Finish:** Face-ready
- **Reference:** ENG-MEM-T07 — DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18827106
