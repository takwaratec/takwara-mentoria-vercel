## 3. Work Safety and Biosafety

Real innovation is responsible. We avoid reproducing claims that the plant is "100% harmless" in any productive scenario.

- **Acre Study (2025): Carcinogenic Toxicity of Bamboo Shavings.**
  Peer-reviewed study on respiratory risks and the potential carcinogenicity of shavings and fine dust generated by industrial bamboo processing and sanding. Reinforces the absolute obligation of PPE use and cyclonic exhaust systems in manufacturing (as in the Takwara Tamboreador equipment).

## 4. The Heart of the Technology: Vegetal Polyurethane

- **São Carlos Institute of Chemistry (USP) — Dr. Gilberto Orivaldo Chierice:**
  All the technical basis of "Bio-Weld" derives from decades of scientific research on Castor Oil (*Ricinus communis*). The literature on the use of this polymer in biomedical prostheses (bones and skulls) supports our argument of toxic inertia (perfect curing without emission of harmful VOCs).
