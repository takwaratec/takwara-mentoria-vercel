## The Greenwashing Scam

> *"Science and technology are not the same thing. The dangers of technology without ethics are concrete. They sell wood and bamboo treated with formaldehyde, CCA, and CCB as 'sustainable construction.' That's not a bio-solution — that's greenwashing."*
>
> — **Miguel Nicolelis**, Programa 20 Minutos, May/2026

**CCA treatment** injects arsenic, chromium, and copper into autoclaves. **CCB** replaces arsenic with boron, which accumulates in groundwater. **Formaldehyde** in plywood, MDF, and panels is a **WHO-recognized carcinogen**.

None of these three should be inside a home. Yet they are — with a green seal on the package.

### Chinese BLC: the unregulated bamboo

Brazil imports tons of **Bamboo Laminated Board (BLC)** from China — panels, flooring, furniture — made with **urea-formaldehyde adhesive**. They arrive with zero ANVISA, INMETRO, or MAPA certification. No recall. No press coverage.

A Brazilian developer trying to produce the same material with non-toxic glue or Vegetable PU faces endless requirements: lab tests, emissions assays, factory certification, NBR 16828 compliance. The Chinese BLC importer faces none.

**The global paradox:** VOCs banned in the EU, US, and Japan are freely produced in China and dumped in the Global South. The third world has become the poison dump that the first world banned.
