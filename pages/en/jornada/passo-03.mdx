## 0. Context — Why This Format

Tecnologia Takwara is a grassroots innovation validated by 15 years of prototyping and recognized by the Nature Awards curation, but **without formal academic ties**. The institutional funding system did not embrace it — CEFET's incubator suggested shifting focus to "bamboo + asphalt."

**Direct consequence:** Mentorship is not just a product. It is the **viralization vehicle** of the technology. To survive and scale, it must be independent, demonstrable, and built community by community.

This format was designed considering:

- 60 years of age, spinal injury, comorbidities → workshop hours are the scarcest resource
- 15 years of prototypes, time-lapses, and unpublished photos → giant visual archive not yet published
- DIY content as flagship → "see how it's done" is worth more than theory
- Imperveg partnership under negotiation → physical kit as entry point
- Deadline: July 18, 2026

## 3. Touchpoints

| Where | Purpose | Frequency | Format |
|:------|:--------|:----------|:-------|
| **Site (Nextra/GitHub Pages)** | Public content, TECs, blog | Continuous updates | Written + diagrams |
| **YouTube** | Demo videos, behind the scenes, lessons | 1-2x/week | Video (5-15 min) |
| **WhatsApp/Telegram** (group) | Community, quick questions, notices | Daily (async) | Text + audio |
| **Email** | Enrollment, materials, structured support | Weekly | Newsletter + PDFs |
| **Live session (Zoom/Meet)** | Entry Mentorship — collective Q&A | Biweekly | 60 min, recorded |
| **Technical Visit** | High-Ticket Consulting | By appointment | In person |

### Golden rule of touchpoints

> **Your time is the scarcest resource.**
> Async content (site + YouTube + wiki) solves 80% of questions without you repeating the same answer.
> Synchronous sessions (live, visits) are reserved for those who have already completed the Entry Mentorship.

## 5. Insights for Step 4 — Accelerators

The program material asks: *"what are the bottleneck moments in your mentee's journey?"*

Applying to Takwara Mentoring, we identify these **likely bottlenecks** and accelerator suggestions:

| Bottleneck Moment | Accelerator (Step 4) | Format |
|:------------------|:---------------------|:-------|
| "I don't know where to start" | **Checklist "What to buy before starting"** with links | PDF + site page |
| "My first PU mix went wrong" | **Pot-Life Calculator** (temperature × ratio) | Simple web tool |
| "I don't know if my bamboo is dry enough" | **Color and texture chart** (visual reference) | Infographic + video |
| "The kiln is expensive, I can't afford it" | **Alternative treatment guide without kiln** (immersion + jungle curing) | PDF |
| "I need proof before investing" | **T-Series Project Portfolio (T01-T12)** — Amazon prospections with scientific backing | Site page |
| "I can't drill the bamboo node without cracking it" | **Video "3 ways to drill the node"** (time-lapse) | YouTube Short |
| "My dome came out crooked" | **Assembly jig template** in printable PDF | PDF + tutorial |
| "I need certification for the work" | **Technical report template** + applicable standards list (ABNT, NBR) | Editable document |

**These accelerators will be formally developed in Step 4.**
