# Step 1: Your Diagnosis and Profile in Bioconstruction

**Mentoring Journey — Tecnologia Takwara**

---

## 1. The Starting Point

In this first step of your journey, it is essential to identify your profile. Tecnologia Takwara was designed to serve everyone from the enthusiast building in their own garage to entire communities that depend on durable infrastructure in the forest.

Knowing your profile helps us direct the best application of the **Bio-Solder Kit** and our treatment techniques to your reality.

---

## 2. Which profile fits you?

### The Bioconstructor / Ecological Architect (Professional Enablement)

- **Your pain:** You need strong, sustainable, waterproof connectors for large-scale bioconstruction projects, but you refuse to use highly toxic epoxy resins.
- **What you seek:** Technical validation and autonomy to incorporate the "vegetable solder" into your service portfolio, delivering durable works (20 to 50 years) with an *Eco-Luxury* standard.

### The "Maker" and DIY Garage Artisan (Creative Scale)

- **Your pain:** You build furniture, domes, bicycle frames, or design objects in your backyard, but you can't access high-performance materials because the industry only sells in giant drums.
- **What you seek:** A fractionated kit, ready to use, with a foolproof step-by-step guide for mixing and injecting Vegetable Polyurethane correctly.

### The Permaculturist / Ecovillage Manager (Autonomous Community)

- **Your pain:** You have abundant access to native bamboo, but your constructions rot quickly due to lack of proper treatment and efficient connections.
- **What you seek:** Structured knowledge to empower your community to build greenhouses, housing, and sheds that last — without depending on industrial products that harm the soil.

### The Territory Guardians and Rooted Permaculturists (Soul of the Forest)

- **Your pain:** You live in extreme humidity environments and need durable infrastructure for your village, Indigenous Land, or Extractive Reserve, but you reject contamination by poisons (CCA/CCB) in bamboo.
- **What you seek:** A 100% clean, safe, non-toxic technology that respects the ancestral territory and guarantees sanitation and structural durability.

## 3. Our Qualification Matrix (Operational Transparency)

Since our capacity for follow-up is limited, we use an internal matrix ("Lead Scoring") to prioritize who receives direct support from Takwara Mentoring.

**Profile Evaluation Table** (scores 1 to 5):

| Personas | Authority / Connection | Value Return | Purchasing Power | Pain Latency | Total |
|:---------|:---------------------:|:------------:|:----------------:|:------------:|:-----:|
| **P1** (Architect) | 4 | 5 | 5 | 4 | **18** |
| **P2** (DIY Maker) | 5 | 3 | 3 | 3 | **14** |
| **P3** (Ecovillage) | 5 | 4 | 2 | 5 | **16** |
| **P4** (Purists / Indigenous) | 5 | 5 | 1 | 5 | **16** |

### Why We Made These Choices

- **Persona 1 (The Enabler):** The technical pain is very high (risk of losing contracts if the work cracks or rots). Value return is immediate upon mastering the "vegetable solder."
- **Persona 2 (The Scale):** Essential for spreading *maker* culture. The focus here is not on extended follow-up, but on quick access to fractionated kits.
- **Persona 3 (The Community):** Total philosophical alignment with Takwara Technology, empowering the territory to not depend on conventional industry.
- **Persona 4 (The Soul of the Project):** The need is maximum and the connection is absolute due to the zero toxicity of our resin. The sponsorship and grant model enables the technology to reach places without purchasing power.
